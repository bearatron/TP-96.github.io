Tile Scale
How to Play
  • Arrow keys or WASD move all tiles simultaneously.
  • Two tiles of the same value merge into one.
  • Each merge scores points equal to the resulting tile.
  • After every move, a 2 or 4 tile spawns in an empty cell.
  • Hit 2048 to win — but you can keep playing for a higher score.
  • The game ends when the board is full and no merges are possible.
Controls
  • Arrow Up / W — slide tiles up.
  • Arrow Down / S — slide tiles down.
  • Arrow Left / A — slide tiles left.
  • Arrow Right / D — slide tiles right.
  • Undo reverts the last move (5 undos per game).
  • Swipe on mobile to move tiles in that direction.
